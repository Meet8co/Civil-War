Welcome to Camp!

Hello, recruit! Are you ready to be part of something great? Life as a soldier is challenging, exciting, and full of adventure. We are looking for brave individuals like you to join our ranks!

In this guide, you will learn what to expect, from training and battles to the meals we have in camp. By the end, you will be prepared to grab your gear and march with us!

Training

You should expect training to be challenging and rigorous. The training will include learning how to march, practice loading and firing weapons, conducting drills, and following orders.

Here is an example of a daily training schedule:

  • Marching for hours everyday in formations, such as columns and creating a "company front."
  • Learning how to move in straight lines as a group and quickly.
  • Learning how to set up tents and dig trenches quickly.

Uniforms

Union soldiers wear blue, and Confederate soldiers wear gray. The uniforms are made of heavy wool, with leather-billed caps and stiff shoes.

  • You will wear a four-button "sack" coat over your cotton or flannel shirt, and trousers held by suspenders.
  • The coat will have bright buttons, piping, or badges to indicate your rank.
  • The Hardee hat, a wide-brimmed dress hat, is the regulation for the Union army.
  • Confederate soldiers preferred the Slouch hat for its comfort, protection from the elements, and versatility.
  • All soldiers preferred the ankle-high leather shoe called Brogan.

Health & Hygiene

Let me be honest, camp life isn’t always clean. But if you’re tough, you’ll be just fine!

  • We don’t always have real bathrooms or clean water.
  • Sometimes, sickness spreads fast, like typhoid or dysentery.
  • But if you keep yourself clean and smart, you’ll stay healthy!
  • Don’t worry, everyone deals with a few bugs now and then.
  • We will provide hospitals, medical treatment, and nurses.
  • Stay strong, and you'll survive anything and everything camp throws at you!

Battles

Ready to show off your bravery? Battles are where legends are made!

  • You’ll march into battles.
  • We use drums and flags to move together, even through all the smoke.
  • It’s loud and crazy, but when we win, it feels incredible!
  • We fight in open fields, with no places to hide, relying solely on our courage and teamwork.
  • Stick with your friends, listen to your officer, and you’ll do great.

Camp Life

When were not fighting, camp is our home. You’ll will love it there!

  • We eat hardtack crackers, beans, and salty meat, it keeps you strong!
  • We pass time by writing letters, playing cards, and telling jokes.
  • The tents aren’t fancy, but after a long day, they will feel cozy.
  • If you’re handy, you can build wooden shelters!
  • And we have chaplains who keep our spirits high when we miss home.
